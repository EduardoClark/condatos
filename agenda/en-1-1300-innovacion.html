13:00-14:00 PM


The Internet of Things


Speakers

Vagner Diniz | World Wide Web Consortium | BR
André Pase | Pontifícia Universidade Católica do Rio Grande do Sul | BR
Erik Huesca* | Academia Mexicana de Informática + FUNCO | MX


Moderator

Caroline Burle


Anfitriones Co-hosts
W3C

Currently there are more devices conected to the internet than people in the world. In 2020 there will be 50 billion connected devices. This requieres web accessible, machine readable data in order for devices to connect among each other. This panel will present an conceptual approximation to the subject, followed by practical cases of Internet of Things and the presentation of IFTT technology which allow technology automation using Web tools that interact with connected hardware.

Tourism requires the productive use of information and applied research for the design efficient of public policies and generation of business models that make tourism destinations attractive. For this, it is necessary to use sources to generate a discussionon the possibility to approximate the behavior of tourists with data and provide knowledge based on real activity, not just surveys. This analysis requires the use of open data for tourism as well as strong cooperation between the public, private and social sectors.

Making Policy Public


EGAP


October 1st from 11:00 AM to 5:30 PM


Aimed at decision-makers, public administrators and government advisors to explore the use of open data to improve the performance of governments and raise the quality of decision-making in public policy. It focuses on improving the professional skills of the participants in the use of open data for government solutions.

By the Graduate School of Public Administration and Public Policy at ITESM

Open Contracts and data standardization in public procurement


Speakers

Alejandro Luna
José M. Alonso
Marcela Rozo
Ania Calderón


Moderator

Felipe Estefan


Anfitriones Co-hosts
WB

The aim of this panel is to discuss the importance of implementing innovative practices for the use of open data in promoting greater transparency by governments and greater involvement of multiple stakeholders in the public procurement process.

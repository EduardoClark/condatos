15:00-16:00 PM


Data Revolution for Post-2015


Speakers

Emma Samman | The Open Data Institute | UK
Emmanuel Letouze | Data Pop Alliance
María Gabriela Porro | Open Aid Partnership + Banco Mundial


Moderator

Philipp Schönrock


Anfitriones Co-hosts
CEPEI

This panel's objective is to discuss and evaluate the positive spillovers open data could have for sustainable development; also to look at the main challenges and opportunities of open data use and how they'll contribute to the objectives of post-2015 development.

Similarities and differences between open data and access to public information


Speakers

Haydee Pérez | Fundar | MX
Romina Colman | La Nación Data | AR
Fabiano Angélico | Prefectura de la Ciudad de Sao Paolo | BR


Moderator

Silvana Fumega


Anfitriones Co-hosts
Silvana Fumega

It is necessary to explore the convergence and divergence between actors who work in areas of open government data and access tu public information within a same government to understand areas of collaboration. This panel will promote a dialogue and exchange of perspectives between different actors and sector with the objectove of advancing the open data agenda in the region.

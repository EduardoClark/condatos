Open Data 101


The Open Data Institute


October 1st


Session 1 : 1:00 PM to 2:00 PM

Session 2: 4:00 PM to 6:00 PM

The objective of this workshop given by the ODI (theodi.org) is to learn what open data and the alternatives for its publication. There will be practical exercises conducted to learn what is and what is not open data, learn about open data's potential benefits, risks, and how they could impact government processes. Register for this workshop if you want to learn more about open data from the leading organization on open data education and advocacy .

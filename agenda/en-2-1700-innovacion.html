17:00-18:00 PM


Where's the 3 trillion?


Speakers

Mat Travizano | Grandata | US
Santiago Siri | Partido de la Red | AR
Marc Dacosta | Enigma | US
Rudi Borrmann | Gobierno de la Ciudad de Buenos Aires | AR


Moderator

Alejandro Maza


Anfitriones Co-hosts
OPI

There are still few examples of the 3 trillion dollars in annual economic value Open Data is supposed to generate. This panel will open up the discussion on where the opportunities and challenges to generate this value lie for entrepreneurs up for the challenge. The panelists, who are experienced in generating this value from the trenches, will bring an ambitious yet realistic vision of what the future might look like.

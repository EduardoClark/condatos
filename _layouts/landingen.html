Con Datos

Open Data Regional Conference Latin America and the Caribbean

OCT 1—2 | 2014 Mexico City

ConDatos is the most important regional event on open data in Latin America and the Caribbean.

This year, Mexico City will be hosting academics, governments, business and civil society organizations so they can interact in discussions, roundtables and workshops to enrich the debate of open data on different topics.
